WORKSHOP 2017

The research and robotics group of MNIT, ZINE, conducts a workshop every year for the freshers in order to get them acquainted with the basics of various fields like Aeromodelling, Android App Development, Website Development, Ethical Hacking, Electronics and so much more.

The aim of the workshop is to light the fire of curiosity in the minds of freshers and also to choose a few of them to be part of the ZINE fraternity.
